Clippy

A lightweight, customizable browser extension designed to help you manage notes and summarize web content quickly and efficiently.
Features

    Quick Summary: Instantly capture and summarize text from any webpage.

    Sticky Notes: Keep quick, persistent notes pinned to your screen while you browse.

    Full Customization: Tailor the UI to your style:

        Custom colors for the header, buttons, and background.

        Configurable text and input area styles.

    Floating & Resizable: Move and resize your Clippy window to fit your workflow.

Installation

    1 Clone this repository to your local machine:
    git clone https://github.com/domi021/clippy.git
    
    2. Open your browser (Chrome/Zen) and go to your extensions management page (e.g., `chrome://extensions/`).

    3. Enable **Developer mode** in the top-right corner.
     
    4. Click **Load unpacked** and select the `clippy` folder you just cloned.

## Usage
* Click the **Clippy icon** in your browser toolbar to launch the interface.
* Use the **Gear (⚙️) icon** to open the settings menu and adjust your theme colors.
* Use the **Summary** button to scan the page for text.
