Clippy

A lightweight, customizable browser extension designed to help you manage notes and summarize web content quickly and efficiently.
Features

    Quick Summary: Instantly capture and summarize text from any webpage.

    Sticky Notes: Keep quick, persistent notes pinned to your screen while you browse.

    Full Customization: Tailor the UI to your style:

        Custom colors for the header, buttons, and background.

        Configurable text and input area styles.

    Floating & Resizable: Move and resize your Clippy window to fit your workflow.
